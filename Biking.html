Biking

Why i like biking?

I love biking because i feel free and when you're going down a hill you can feel the air flying through your hair and you pass several cars on the way down the hill. You can also get alot out of it, such as; exercise, meeting up with friends, or even biking with your family.

Fun Facts About Biking: German Inventor Karl von Drais is credited with developing the first bicycle. His machine, known as the "swiftwalker," hit the road in 1817. This early bicycle had no pedals, and its frame was a wooden beam. Another fun fact about bikes is, that the first ever bike were 60" high wheelers and they sold for around 125 dollars which in pounds it would be 90 pounds and 64 pence. Moreover, the very first bike had a wooden frame with steel tires for it's wheels.
